About Me

I still remember the first time my parents brought a computer to our house. It was a Toshiba laptop that my dad used for work. It took forever to turn on, so we had to schedule some time to wait for it to start.

Since that moment I got hooked. My favorite courses in school were computer-related ones. I even did a web page about Egypt as a science fair project.

The pattern kept going during college and my first few internships. I started using SQL and Python to perform complex analysis tasks in a relative short timeframe.

Since that moment I haven't stop learning, every chance I got I was studying new languages. Finally, I made the decison to do this full time.

Now my focus is with JavaScript and web development. I'm looking for a full time role where I can apply these new skills to help a company achieve their goals.
