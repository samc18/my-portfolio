About Me

Hello there! I'm Sam and love building things with code.

I still remember the first time my parents brought a computer to our house. It was a Toshiba laptop that my dad used for work. It took forever to turn on, so we had to schedule some time to wait for it to start.

Since that moment I got hooked. My favorite courses in school were computer-related ones. I even did a web page about Egypt as a science fair project.

Time passed and I got to college. Here is where things change a bit. I went for an Industrial Engineering degree. But same with school I always did better in classes that involved computers, but these were now about programming. As an industrial engineer computers weren't the main focus but the passion still remained

During my first internships, I was in charge of creating different reports. Some of them were easy to make others a bit more complex and time consuming. That's when the spark was reignated. I decide to learn a bit more about programming languages mainly focused on spreadsheets like VBA, M language and DAX. It not only made my life a lot easier but I became a better employee since I could accomplished really hard tasks within a relative short timeframe.

Since that moment I haven't stop learning, I gained experience with SQL and Python for data analysis purposes. Finally I made the decison to do this full time and not only use it as a tool.

Now my focus is with JavaScript and web development. I'm looking for a full time role where I can apply these new skills to help a company achieve their goals.
